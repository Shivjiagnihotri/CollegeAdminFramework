Shankarprasad Agnihotri College of Engineering, Wardha was established under Agnihorti Group of Institution. It was established in 1985 by Pandit Shri. Shankar Prasadji Agnihotri as a result of dedication and motivation of late Smt. Ranibai Agnihotri who was inspired by the father of the nation Mahatma Gandhi and Vinoba Bhave. It’s now grown up with more than 20 educational units of various kinds, right from pre-primary schools to post graduate institution.
